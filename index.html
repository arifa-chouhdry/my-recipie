Badam ka Halwa

by chef Zakir

Ingredients

  • Almond past 1 cup
  • Clarified butter 1 cup
  • Semolina 250 grams
  • Milk 2 cups
  • Sugar Â½ cup
  • Khoya 250 grams
  • Almond essence 1 tsp
  • Almond for garnish

Cooking direction

  1. Put almonds in blender with a little water and blend to make a paste.

  2. Melt clarified butter, add semolina and roasted until an aroma rises. Then add milk and add sugar and almond paste mix it well and stir fry.

  3. Melt clarified butter, add semolina and roasted until an aroma rises. Then add milk and add sugar and almond paste mix it well and stir fry.

  4. Take it out in a serving platter and garnish with almonds and serve it.
